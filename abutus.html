ABOUT US

Scoops of Happiness, a division of Ice-cream Limited (Patna), is Patna's favorite ice-cream and discussion destination. We make every effort to give our visitors with the best possible experience. Thousands of people helped us become who we are today, and we're grateful to be a part of their lives. In 1996, we launched our first ice cream parlour on Patna's Boring Road, and it quickly became one of the city's most popular hangouts for the young and the young at heart. Since then, it's been an amazing adventure to become the state's largest structured retail shop chain. We're also located in several regions of Bihar and Uttar Pradesh, so if your travels take you there, stop by one of our locations for a taste of home!
